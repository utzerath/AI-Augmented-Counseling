MAST

0. Do you enjoy a drink now and then?
1. Do you feel you are a normal drinker or drug user? Normal means you drink or use less than or about the same as most other people and you have not gotten into recurring trouble while drinking.
2. Have you ever awakened after drinking or drug use and found that you could not remember a part of what happened?
3. Does anyone ever worry or complain about your drinking or drug use?
4. Can you stop drinking or drug use without a struggle after one or two drinks or uses?
5. Do you feel guilty about your drinking or drug use?
6. Do friends or relatives think you are a normal drinker or drug user?
7. Are you able to stop drinking or drug use when you want to?
8. Have you ever attended a meeting of Alcoholics Anonymous or any other 12 step meetings?
9. Have you gotten into physical fights when you have been drinking or using drugs?
10. Has your drinking or drug use ever created problems between you and your parents, another relative, your spouse, or any girlfriend of boyfriend?
11. Has any family member of yours ever gone for help about your drinking or drug use?
12. Have you ever lost friends because of your drinking or drug use?
13. Have you ever gotten into trouble at work or school because of drinking and drug use?
14. Have you ever lost your job because of drinking and drug use?
15. Have you ever neglected your obligations (school, work, family, or job) for 2 or more days in a row because you were drinking or using?
16. Do you drink or use drugs before noon fairly often?
17. Have you ever been told you have liver trouble? Cirrhosis?
18. After heavy drinking or drug use, have you ever had severe shaking, or heard voices or seen things that really werent there?
19. Have you ever gone to anyone (education or treatment) for help about your drinking or drug use?
20. Have you ever been in a hospital (injury or illness) because of drinking or drug use?
21. Have you ever been a patient in a psychiatric hospital or on a psychiatric ward of a general hospital where drinking or drug use was part of the problem that resulted in hospitalization?
22. Have you ever been to a psychiatric or mental health clinic or gone to any doctor, social worker, or clergy for help with any emotional problem where drinking or drug use was part of the problem
23. Have you ever been cited, arrested for drunk driving, driving while intoxicated, or driving under the influence (DUI) of alcoholic beverages or any other drug?
24. Other than DUI, have you ever been cited, arrested, or taken into custody, for possession or use of alcohol, medication, other drugs or paraphernalia?

ADHS

Part A

1. Have you had 3 or more alcohol/drug related arrests/citations?
2. Was your blood alcohol level .15 or above?
3. Do you habitually abuse or have a problem controlling alcohol or drugs?
4. Do you feel you have a problem with alcohol/drugs?
5. Have you been diagnosed with substance abuse or organic brain disease due to substance abuse?
6. Have you experienced any of these withdrawal symptoms:

a. Hallucinogens
b. Convulsive Seizures
c. Delirium tremens
7. Have been medically diagnosed as having any of these complications?

a. Alcohol liver disease
b. Alcoholic pancreatitis
c. Alcoholic cardiomyopathy

Part B

1. Have you had two alcohol/drug related arrests/citations?
2. Have you ever lost time from work or school due to alcohol or drug use?
3. Have you had any problems with family, friends or peers due to your alcohol/drug use?
4. Have you had 3 or more alcohol/drug related arrests/citations?
5. Have you ever gone to a DUI class, Alcohol/Drug class, medical facility, 6. ___ ___ or treatment center because of your alcohol/drug use?
6. Have you ever had any memory loss after alcohol/drug use?
7. Have you ever passed out (become unconscious) after drinking/drug use?
8. Have you experienced any of these after alcohol/drug use?

a. Shakes or malaise relived by drinking/using
b. Irritability
c. Nausea
d. Anxiety
9. Have you used alcohol/drugs to cope with or escape from problems/stress?
10. Have you experienced an increase of use, a change of tolerance (e.g. BAC .18) or change in pattern of use?
11. Have you ever had dramatic personality changes after alcohol/drug use?
